Rules & Regulations

Tournament Guidelines

  1. 1.

    Vicky Ball matches will be played.

  2. 2.

    Runs will be considered all over the field.

  3. 3.

    Tree pitch will not count towards the boundary or catches.

  4. 4.

    11 players per match, with 2 substitutes allowed.

  5. 5.

    Umpire's decision is final. If a decision is wrong, only captains are allowed to speak to the umpire.

  6. 6.

    Live scoreboard will be available on the CricHeroes Application.

  7. 7.

    6 overs per innings (7 overs per innings for finals).

  8. 8.

    Every innings will be played with a new ball.

  9. 9.

    No-ball (free-hit), wide-ball & over-throw will carry runs as per cricket rules.

  10. 10.

    A player who has played in one team is not allowed to play in another team.

  11. 11.

    Team players and all details have to be submitted 1-2 days before the match day.

  12. 12.

    Match scheduling will be shared 1 day before.
